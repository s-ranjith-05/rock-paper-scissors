Rock-Paper-Scissors Rules

Objective:

The goal of Rock-Paper-Scissors is to select an option that defeats the computer's choice based on the following rules:

  • Rock crushes Scissors: If you choose Rock and the computer chooses Scissors, you win!
  • Scissors cuts Paper: If you choose Scissors and the computer chooses Paper, you win!
  • Paper covers Rock: If you choose Paper and the computer chooses Rock, you win!

How to Play:

  1. Select either Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. The computer will randomly select one of the three options.
  3. The winner is determined based on the rules listed above.
  4. If both you and the computer choose the same option, it's a tie, and the round is replayed.
